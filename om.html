THE STORY BEHIND SUSHI HOUSE

Told by Hana Kaninki, owner and chef.

It all started when I – as a child – had a great interest and passion for food. Because of this it did not take me long to decide that I wanted to become a chef. Initially, this led to me educating from French cuisine. When I had served my apprenticeship as a chef, I had a burning desire to be independent but I did not want to have any investors involved; I wanted to succeed on my own. I therefore announced that I would open a sushi restaurant in Hokkaido but I was met with scepticism from my surroundings. This did not bother me, on the contrary; the more people doubted, the more I wanted it. Originally, the restaurant was to be located in Tokyo where I come from but it soon proved to be beyond economic reach since I was only in the early 20s and without any savings.
